Welcome to the Amstel Campus of the University of Applied Sciences of Amsterdam! We’re excited to have you here. The campus is a vibrant hub for learning, featuring eight modern educational buildings designed to support diverse fields of study. Among these are the inspiring Kohnstamm building and the Muller-Lulofs building, each offering state-of-the-art facilities to enhance your academic journey. As you explore the campus, you’ll find spaces that foster collaboration, innovation, and creativity, all set in the heart of Amsterdam. We hope you enjoy your time here and make the most of everything this dynamic campus has to offer!
